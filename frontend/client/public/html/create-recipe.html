Tips for uploading a quality recipe:

  1. A descriptive title. "Grandma's Casserole" doesn't tell us much, but "Grandma's Spinach and Cream Chicken Casserole" does!
  2. Accurate Measurements. We know that, often, family recipes are "a pinch of this" and "a bunch of that" but our advice is to take measurements the next time you make it to update amounts so others can enjoy it exactly as intended.
  3. Add your family's story. We want to hear why this recipe is special to you and yours to help bring us closer.
