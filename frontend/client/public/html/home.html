Grab a Plate!

If you are here, you are like us. You love food. We created Everybody's Favorite Plate based of the fond memories we all have of our favorite family meal. For one, it is the grilled cheese their wife makes every Tuesday with 1 slice of tomato and 3 strips of bacon. For another, it is their grandmother's homemade tamales. And for yet another, it is the 4th generation sticky buns and the long standing family debate on whether nuts should be added. Food brings us together, and everyone's family recipes shared here can help heal this world just a little bit more, bite by bite. Feel free to save recipes, upload your own, and start cooking!
