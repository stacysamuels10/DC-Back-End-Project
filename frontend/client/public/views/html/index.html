Welcome to Everyone's Favorite Plate

Crowdsourcing the family dinner table.

We want to be a resource for people to not lose their precious recipes. Whether it is the 100 year old secret recipe from your great-grandmother or the recipe you and your partner made up and eat every Tuesday night, we know the dinner table brings people together. Experiencing each others lives through the food on our plates can bring the world a little bit closer. The key to each of these recipes is they all have one secret ingredient in common:

Love

So take a browse and see the recipes that are Everyone's Favorite Plate.
